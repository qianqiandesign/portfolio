BACKGROUND

When I first joined the company one year ago, we had two business mode, one is the short term rental management for house owners and the other one is the short term platform for apartment residents.With different growth speeds, we saw the resident mode became the leading and trending growth. The company decided to shift focus from house owner based operation team to the apartment resident based on the cooperation with the property owners.


PROPOSAL

With business changes, we knew our existing dashboard and process of the onboarding flow would not meet the needs for the users. This means each role in the business chain, the resident, the community manager, and the property manager, will rely more on the dashboard to manage the program and they need seperated features and functionalities.

TARGET AUDIENCE

Apartment residents living in Pillow Communities

Community manager of apartments who need to manager the guests and the residents

Portfolio & regional Managers who need to manager the guests and the residents
