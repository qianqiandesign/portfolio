BACKGROUND

I've been a fan of instant noodles all my life and in 2016, decided to turn this passion into a website. While living in Seattle alone, I've found Asian grocery stores aplenty and my love affair with the instant noodle has only increased. Over the years, I've found some pretty amazing varieties and I want to turn the list which represents the best of the best into a website to spread my love of the instant noodle to the world.

PROPOSAL

The purpose of the website is to introduce the instant noodle to the world. While instant noodles were born in Japan, they have now integrated into the diets of many different of countries and have taken root in as instant noodles of their own. I want to show how people enjoy instant noodle and provide the information to start your journey to ramen today.

TARGET AUDIENCE

Age Range / Generation Identifier and Behavioral Attribute

People in bay area who are new to instant noodle and want to learn more about it

Instant noodle lovers in bay area who want to find out further information andy find ramen store near them
