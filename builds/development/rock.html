BACKGROUND

When I first joined the company one year ago, we had two business mode, one is the short term rental management for house owners and the other one is the short term platform for apartment residents.With different growth speeds, we saw the resident mode became the leading and trending growth. The company decided to shift focus from house owner based operation team to the apartment resident based on the cooperation with the property owners.

PROPOSAL

This means more efforts and features about the platform were added to the homepage. With business changes, we knew our existing website design and process of the onboarding flow would not meet the needs for the team and satisfying our users.
